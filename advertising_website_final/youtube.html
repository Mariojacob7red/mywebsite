Nintendo Retro©

The Nintendo Retro™ is a revolutionary new console that can play any game you like from the Nintendo 64™ to the Nintendo Switch™ all in one compact place.

This video was made in respect to Satoru Iwata, late president of Nintendo. He passed away on July 11, 2015, at the age of 55, due to colon cancer.
