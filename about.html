My Background

I grew up in Western Arkansas in a place called Greenwood. Even though I only lived there until I was 4 years old, I still consider it home. I have been moving around from house to house growing up with my family, and the total has reached 10 times that I have moved into different houses. All in all, I'm glad my family finally found the perfect place to call home in Branson, Missouri.

My Hobbies

I have a lot of hobbies and passions that I won't be able to list them all. One particular hobbie I have is that I love playing video games! Especially if they are Mario games made by Nintendo.

I make YouTube videos in my free time and I've been doing it for the past 10 years, since April 26, 2012. I don't do it for the money. I do it because I have fun making videos for my viewers to watch and it's great that I can bring happiness to anybody that manages to find my videos online! :D

Back in September of 2020, I became the director of the club, Horizon's Project, on campus. At first, I thought it was going to be stressful being in charge of a club, but after I started planning club activities and getting to play video games over SBU's Twitch channel, I found out it was really fun! I was sad to have to leave the club when I graduated from SBU, but I still plan to help them out any way I can for the next few years to come!




One of my other hobbies includes playing tennis. I love playing it with other people too when I get a chance. Now that I think about it, I grew up playing a lot of sports. The only one I haven't played before is soccer.

My Skills

I love using programs like Adobe Photoshop and I'm starting to get really good at using Adobe InDesign. I've used Adobe Illustrator a few times before in the past, but I need to use it more to get acquainted with the software once again. I'm really good at using Cyberlink PowerDirector 8. It's the program I've used to make every video on my YouTube channel and I'd like to try out other software someday too when I can afford it.
